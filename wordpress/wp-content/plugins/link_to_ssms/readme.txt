Post_views is a Simple Wordpress Plugin to show the views till date, of various posts and pages of a blog.

== Description ==

 * Wordpress Plugin for viewing <b>Number of views</b> for the Posts and Pages of a Blog.
 * This simple Plugin <b>Helps the Admins</b> to get the Info about the Views till date of various posts on the Blog in a form of Table Chart.
 * This may help them to Judge their various Authors and may also <b>get the Idea of the tastes of their readers</b>.

== Installation ==

= From your WordPress dashboard =

1. Visit 'Plugins > Add New'
2. Search for 'Post_views'
3. Activate Post_views from your Plugins page.

= From WordPress.org =

1. Download Post_views
2. Upload the 'post_views-new' directory to your '/wp-content/plugins/' directory, using your favorite method (ftp, sftp, scp, etc...)
3. Activate Post_views from your Plugins page.
